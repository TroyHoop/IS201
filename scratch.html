Why I Love Data & Analytics

As an accounting student at BYU, I've learned that numbers tell stories. Whether it's financial statements or basketball statistics, data analysis reveals insights that aren't immediately obvious. Basketball analytics combines two of my passions: sports and quantitative analysis.

The evolution from basic stats (points, rebounds, assists) to advanced metrics (PER, True Shooting %, BPM) mirrors the evolution in accounting from simple bookkeeping to complex financial modeling and predictive analytics.
